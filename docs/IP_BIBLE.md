# SkyWarrior IP Bible

Original intellectual property for SkyWarrior. No Ace Combat names, factions, or story beats are used.

---

## Setting

**Year:** 2041  
**Theater:** The **Meridian Strait** — a narrow sea corridor between the continental powers of **Valden** (west) and **Kharos** (east).  
**Conflict:** The **Ashford Conflict** — Kharos blockades Meridian shipping lanes after Valden recognizes the breakaway **Free Port of Lannis**. Valden responds with a limited air campaign codenamed **Operation Skyline**.

---

## Nations

### Valden Republic
- Democratic federation, blue-gold roundel (single chevron).
- Primary player allegiance.
- Military doctrine: precision strike, AWACS-heavy, squadron autonomy.

### Kharos Directorate
- Authoritarian state, crimson delta on black.
- Antagonist force; uses massed SAM belts and veteran aces.
- Not cartoon evil — professional military with propaganda narrative.

### Free Port of Lannis (neutral)
- City-state on the strait; mission theater landmark **Lannis Harbor**.

## Player unit

**Squadron:** **Viper Squadron** (Valden 7th Air Wing)  
**Home base:** **Fort Ashford** (coastal airfield, campaign hub)  
**Callsign format:** Viper-1 (player), Viper-2 through Viper-4 (wingmen)

## Tone and narrative

- **Tone:** Cinematic military drama with squad camaraderie; not grimdark, not satire.
- **Themes:** Professionalism under pressure, cost of blockade warfare, squad loyalty.
- **Radio style:** AWACS vectors → wingman color → ace taunt → command override on boss missions.
- **Cutscene approach:** In-engine Sequencer dialogues at Fort Ashford briefing room; no real-world politics.

---

## Legal / brand guardrails

- Do not use "Ace Combat", "Project Aces", Bandai Namco assets, or real aircraft manufacturer logos without license.
- Music: original score only; no homage MIDI rips of copyrighted themes.
- UI: match *layout language* (radar band, lock box) with original art direction — teal-on-charcoal HUD palette for SkyWarrior.
